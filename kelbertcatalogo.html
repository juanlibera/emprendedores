Academic Information

Semester System

Kelbert University operates on a semester system with 8-week sessions throughout the year. Courses are offered in Fall, Spring, and Summer semesters.

Grading System

Undergraduate students must maintain a 2.0 GPA. Graduate students must maintain a 3.0 GPA. Letter grades range from A (94-100%) to F (0-59%).

Transfer Credits

Transfer credit evaluation requires course description and school catalog. Maximum 25% of total credits can be transferred. $350 evaluation fee applies.

University Policies

Academic Integrity Policy

Cheating and plagiarism are strictly prohibited. Cheating includes giving or obtaining aid by illicit means. Plagiarism is using another's work without proper acknowledgment.

Penalties: First offense results in formal documentation. Second offense becomes part of permanent academic record. Subsequent offenses may lead to suspension or dismissal.

Attendance Policy

Students are expected to attend all scheduled classes. More than 2 "free" absences per semester are not permitted.

Consequences:

  • 3-5 absences: One-letter grade deduction from final course grade
  • 6+ absences: Failure of the course regardless of grade average
Student Conduct Code

Students are subject to disciplinary action for misconduct including:

  • Dishonesty, cheating, or plagiarism
  • Theft or damage to property
  • Disruptive or abusive behavior
  • Possession of weapons or illegal substances
  • Violation of university policies
Refund Policy

Cancellation & Refund Schedule:

  • Week 1 (through add/drop): 100% tuition refund (minus $100 registration fee)
  • Week 2: 75% tuition refunded
  • Week 3: 50% tuition refunded
  • Week 4: 25% tuition refunded
  • Week 5+: 0% tuition refunded ($75 withdrawal fee applies)
